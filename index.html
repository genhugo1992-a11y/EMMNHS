🏛️ About Our School

🎯 Mission

To provide quality, equitable, culture-based, and complete basic education where every Filipino is functionally literate, equipped with 21st century skills, and prepared for life-long learning, middle-level skills development, entrepreneurship, or higher education.

🌟 Vision

We dream of Filipinos who passionately love their country and whose values and competencies enable them to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to building the nation.

📖 History

Eduardo M. Moreno National High School was established in 2006 in Binonga-an, San Agustin, Romblon under the Division of Romblon (School ID: 309204).

💎 Core Values

  • Maka-Diyos
  • Maka-tao
  • Makakalikasan
  • Makabansa
  • Excellence & Integrity
  • Community Service

📜 Citizens Charter

This Citizens Charter outlines the services offered by Eduardo M. Moreno NHS and the procedures to avail them.

🏫 A. Principal's Office▼

The principal's office serves in the different aspects of the overall school management which include curriculum and instruction supervision, financial operations, building maintenance, personnel, public relations, school policy, coordination of the instructional program, and other overall school matters. It ensures equal opportunities for all students, teachers, and other program beneficiaries to participate in any project or activity carried out under the DepEd program.

📋 B. Registrar's Office▼

i. Records

School-level record management plays a key role in the overall functioning of the education system. The office facilitates release of school forms requested by students following standard procedures.

a. Issuance of the Following School Forms and Certificates
  • Transcript of Records (SF10)
  • Duplicate Copy of SF9 (Report Card)
  • Certification, Authentication, and Verification of Basic Education School Records
  • Reconstructed Copy of Certificate of Completion and Diploma
  • Certification of Non-Issuance of SF10 (Form 137 to any school)
  • Certified True Photocopy of Original Documents

School records necessary for transfer of schools are issued to clients to comply with the requirements of another institution.

ii. Enhanced Basic Education Information System and Learners Information System

a. Correction in the Basic Profile of Learners

Through Learner Information System (LIS), the school aims to accommodate correction and review of basic information of the learners.

b. Approval of Transfer Request

The school intends to accommodate public schools and/or private schools/institutions requests for transferred-out students.

🧭 C. Guidance Office▼
📝
a. Online/Offline Enrolment

The school conducts enrolment process both online and offline to ensure continued access to learning opportunities.

🎓
b. Request for Certificate of Enrolment

Official document confirming attendance and current enrolment of a learner.

📜
c. Certification of Graduates

School document showing the student is a graduate of this school — for CAV or job application purposes.

🔄
d. Certificate of Eligibility to Transfer

Certifies that a student has no pending accountabilities and is eligible for transfer to another institution.

📄
e. Certified True Copy of Documents
  • Certificate of Completion
  • Certificate of Good Moral
  • Certificate of Graduation
✅
f. Certificate of Good Moral Character

Certifies that a student has no offenses and generally fulfills responsibilities as a student.

🪪
g. Request for Replacement of ID

Ensures students receive an identification card with name, ID number, and photo.

💬
h. Counseling and Referral Services

Helps facilitate emotional, social, work, school, and physical health concerns of learners. Referrals are also provided to identify the best way to facilitate further assistance.

💰
i. Preparation of Students' Financial Subsidy Documents

Prepares and submits monthly reports of qualified students to receive the monthly allowance under the city government's special allowance program.

📚
j. Distribution and Retrieval of Self-Learning Modules

SLMs come in printed and digitized forms, designed to allow learners to choose what, how, when, and where to learn.

🩺
k. Consultation Clinic

Addresses challenges in basic education by helping learners improve their learning and catch up on lessons to meet learning standards.

💼 D. Accounting / Finance Office▼

The school facilitates the process of the Obligation Request and Status (ORS) as required by Commission on Audit for certification of allotment and obligation and for future adjustments of expense accounts. The Budget Unit provides certification of availability of appropriation/allotment legally made for the purpose.

👥 E. Human Resources▼
🔍
a. Recruitment of Teacher Applicants

Accommodates teacher applicants and reviews submitted documents based on DepEd hiring guidelines.

📋
b. Processing of Teacher's Application for ERF

Equivalent Record Form indicates educational preparation, training, experience and extra-curricular activities for professional growth — used for upgrading of positions.

📈
c. Processing of STEP Increment

For Teaching and Non-Teaching Personnel.

💵
d. Processing of Salary Adjustment and NCR Activation

For Newly Promoted Teaching and Non-Teaching Personnel.

🆕
e. Processing of Payroll for Newly Hired Personnel

Payroll and NCR Activation for Newly Hired Teaching and Non-Teaching Personnel.

🏅
f. Processing of Payroll of Loyalty Pay

For Teachers and Non-Teaching Personnel.

📑
g. Processing of Other Personnel Services
  • Application for Leave of Absence
  • Application for Change of Marital Status
  • Application for Stop and Deduct (Payslip)
  • Application for Certificate of Employment with Compensation
  • Application for Permit to Teach / Permit to Study
  • Maternity and Paternity Leave
  • Request for Service Record (SR)
🖥️ F. Custodian / Supply / ICT▼
🏫
a. Online/Offline Request for School Facilities and Equipment

Guides teaching and non-teaching personnel in requesting available school equipment and facilities to maximize their use.

🗂️
b. Request for Office Supplies

The school approves the needed supplies in all offices as stated in the Annual Implementation Plan.

📧
c. Creation/Resetting/Deletion of Students' Division E-mail and Office 365 Accounts

Facilitates requests for creation, resetting, or deletion of Students' Division E-Mail and Office 365 Accounts.

📚 G. Library▼
📖
Borrowing of Books

This service aims to provide books and resources needed by teachers and students as implemented by the school.

🏥 H. Clinic▼
💊
Clinic Services

The school has an organized clinic service which provides assistance for preventive and remedial measures for simple and common ailments and first aid.
