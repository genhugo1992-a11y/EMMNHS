🏛️ About Our School

🎯 Mission

To provide quality, equitable, culture-based, and complete basic education where every Filipino is functionally literate, equipped with 21st century skills, and prepared for life-long learning, middle-level skills development, entrepreneurship, or higher education.

🌟 Vision

We dream of Filipinos who passionately love their country and whose values and competencies enable them to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to building the nation.

📖 History

Eduardo M. Moreno National High School was established in 2006 in Binonga-an, San Agustin, Romblon under the Division of Romblon (School ID: 309204).

💎 Core Values

  • Maka-Diyos
  • Maka-tao
  • Makakalikasan
  • Makabansa
  • Excellence & Integrity
  • Community Service
